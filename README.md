The Goal of this project was 1 main point 

1: make a movement system, basic enough to get from A to B and enough to facilitate a "special move" that i like, also i  wanted to see how hard is it, and understand why movement system are so lackluster in most games, is it because its too hard? too long to make?
2: make a character, something simple low poly, but detailed, the main idea was kinda "PS1-PS2 hybrid anime cellshading"? i had something specific in mind, also extra limbs like a tail, with fur and it needed to animate and deform, and i have to like it...

I also wanted to see how long does something like this take...

There was some sub goal, that became primary after i realised... that i would probably be done before the deadline reach 1/4 of the time i alloted to this

Sub goal 1: a movement system, A to B,  need a A and a B, and the journey in between, i have to make a playground to test its limit and use its mechanic
Sub goal 2: make it look good and package it into a short experience, 3 A to B fetch Quest, few short cutscene, a ending choice, a small narrative personal narrative and world narrative.
